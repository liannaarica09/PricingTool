Welcome!

If your new to this tool here are a few tips:

  1. Start by chosing either the clothing or Non-clothing tab.
  2. Then:
    • For clothing: chose either women's or men's. From the drowpdown menu you can choose a more specific catagory or just search all clothing.
    • For non-clothing: chose to search eBay or Best Buy. Choose the catagory you want to search from the dropdown.
  3. If the item you're pricing is new, check the 'New' box.
  4. Type the brand and any keywords into the search bar and tap the 'Search' button.
  5. A table will appear showing you the number of items matching your search, the price range, and the mean, median, and mode prices.

    If you'd like to see a distribution chart of your results, tap the graph tab.

    If you'd like to look for a specific item or check that your results are accurate tap the list button. If you find the specitic item you can add it to our list of recently priced items by tapping the on picture of the item.
